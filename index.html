DETROIT BECOME HUMAN

About DBH:-

Detroit: Become Human is a 2018 adventure game developed by Quantic Dream. The game was released for the PlayStation 4 in May 2018, published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. A port for Windows was released in December 2019, published by Quantic Dream. The plot follows three androids: Kara (Valorie Curry), who escapes her owner to explore her newfound sentience and protect a young girl; Connor (Bryan Dechart), whose job is to hunt down sentient androids; and Markus (Jesse Williams), who devotes himself to releasing other androids from servitude. The player's choices affect the course and outcome of the story.

Description

Travel to the near-future metropolis of Detroit-a city rejuvenated by an exciting technological development: androids. Witness your brave new world turn to chaos as you take on the role of Kara, a female android trying to find her own place in a turbulent social landscape. Shape an ambitious branching narrative, making choices that will not only determine your own fate, but that of the entire city. Discover what it means to be human from the perspective of an outsider- see the world of man though the eyes of a machine.

Developer names

Detroit: Become Human is a cinematic neo-noir thriller game developed by Quantic Dream and published for PlayStation 4, releasing also for Microsoft Windows. The game is the company's 5th title and their fourth interactive drama. It is written and directed by Quantic Dream's founder and co-CEO David Cage. The game itself was released on May 25, 2018 and the Digital Premium Version was released a day before on May 24, 2018 for PlayStation 4. For Microsoft Windows, it was released on December 12, 2019, as an Epic Games Store exclusive. It received a wider release on the Steam store on June 18, 2020.

Side characters

About Hank

A grizzled and cynical Detroit Police Department lieutenant who becomes Connor's partner. Initially distrustful of androids, his relationship with Connor evolves based on player choices.

About Gavin Reed

A hostile and aggressive detective who dislikes androids, particularly Connor. Often creates tension and conflict in the police department.

About Amanda

Connor's handler and guide within the virtual environment created by CyberLife. Represents CyberLife's interests and assesses Connor's performance.

About Alice

A young girl who Kara cares for and protects. Her safety and well-being are central to Kara's motivations and actions.

About Luther

A large android who assists Kara and Alice. Initially working for Zlatko, he joins Kara and Alice in their journey for freedom.

About Zlatko

A sinister character who deceives and captures deviant androids. Provides a critical turning point in Kara's storyline.

About Rose Chapman

A kind-hearted woman who helps androids escape to Canada. Provides refuge and assistance to Kara and Alice.

About Carl Manfred

An elderly and renowned artist who owns Markus. Treats Markus with kindness and respect, serving as a father figure and inspiring Markus's sense of freedom.

About Leo Manfred

Carl's troubled son who envies Markus and causes significant conflict. Plays a pivotal role in Markus's transformation into a deviant.

About North

A deviant android who becomes one of Markus's closest allies in the revolution. Her background and relationship with Markus can deepen based on player choices

About Josh

Another key member of the deviant revolution, advocating for peaceful methods. His approach contrasts with North's more militant stance.

About Simon

A member of Markus's group who supports the revolution. His fate can vary significantly based on player decisions.
